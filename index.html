DocPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Your documents stay yours.

This policy explains what data DocPilot collects, why, and how it is protected.

Who we are

DocPilot is developed and maintained by Radisa Pajic, based in Vienna, Austria. You can reach us at radisapajic@gmail.com.

What data we collect

  • Your email address and name (via Google Sign-In)
  • Document images and PDF files you upload or scan
  • Text extracted from your documents (OCR)
  • Metadata: document type, date, status, domain, ownership labels you assign
  • App usage data (errors, crashes) via Firebase

Why we collect it

  • To store and sync your documents across sessions
  • To enable AI-powered search and document recognition
  • To send deadline reminders and notifications
  • To allow backup and restore of your documents

AI & third-party services

DocPilot uses the Anthropic Claude API to analyze document images and power smart search. Document content may be sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We also use Google Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, and database services. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or document content with any third parties, except for the service providers listed above (Anthropic, Google Firebase) which are necessary to operate the app.

Data storage & security

Your documents are stored in Google Firebase Cloud Storage, protected by Google's security infrastructure. Access is restricted to your account only. We do not have access to your documents.

Your rights

  • Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of AI analysis (by not using scan features)

To exercise these rights, contact us at radisapajic@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

DocPilot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
